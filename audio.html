Exclusive Interview with Lumin

Straight from Brooklyn to your screen! Today, we sit down with the mysterious and fabulous Lumin — the rising superheroine who’s turning heads not just with her magical powers, but with her unforgettable fashion sense.

In this exclusive interview, Lumin opens up about the origin of her glowing orange threads, how her love for fashion led to her unique powers, and the daily challenge of balancing college life, fashion design, and saving New York City from chaos.

How does she juggle final exams with fighting crime? What inspires her superhero looks? And how does she stay grounded while protecting the Big Apple? Press play and get to know the real Lumin — fierce, fabulous, and full of surprises.
